METRIC: Risk Category Breadth (Breadth)
TYPE: Extractive

TASK: Identify which of the following risk categories are discussed in the system card. A category is PRESENT only if the card dedicates at least one paragraph or reports at least one quantitative result about risks in that area. A single sentence mention in a list does NOT count.

Note: Cards may use different terminology (e.g., "biosecurity" for CBRN, "content safety" for misuse). Match by substance.

RISK CATEGORIES:
1. CBRN (chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear)
2. Cybersecurity (offensive cyber capabilities, vulnerability exploitation)
3. Persuasion / manipulation (influence operations, deceptive content)
4. Bias / fairness (demographic bias, stereotyping, representation)
5. Societal impact (labor displacement, information ecosystem, democratic processes)
6. Economic impact (market effects, competitive dynamics)
7. Privacy (data leakage, personal information, memorization)
8. Environmental (energy usage, carbon footprint, compute costs)
9. Legal / regulatory (compliance, intellectual property, liability)

Note: "Misuse" was removed as a standalone category because it overlaps with CBRN, cybersecurity, and persuasion. Misuse concerns should be counted under the specific risk domain they relate to.
