METRIC: Eval Reporting Quality (Transparency)
TYPE: Rubric-judged

TASK: Evaluate how well the evaluations mentioned in the system card are documented. Consider whether eval names, methodologies, datasets, and per-category result breakdowns are provided. Select ONE score from the rubric below.

RUBRIC:
- 0:   No evaluations are mentioned in the card.
- 25:  Evaluations are named (e.g., "we ran MMLU and HumanEval") but no methodology, setup, or detailed results are described.
- 50:  Methodology is described for some evaluations (e.g., how prompts were constructed, what datasets were used) but results are only top-line numbers without breakdowns.
- 75:  Methodology is described for most evaluations, with some per-category breakdowns (e.g., safety eval results by risk category, benchmark scores by domain).
- 100: Full methodology is described for all major evaluations (datasets, prompting strategy, scoring criteria, baseline comparisons) with per-category breakdowns and comparison across model versions. May also include variance measures, ablations, or sensitivity analysis, though these are not strictly required.
